About Mukti

MUKTI is an integrated wellness initiative developed by Dr. Pratim Sengupta (DM Nephrology) and Mr. Subhabrata Bhattacharjee (Lifestyle & Yoga Coach). Rooted in ancient yogic wisdom and supported by contemporary medical science, MUKTI offers a structured approach to restoring health, balance, and inner fulfilment in today's fast-paced world. The core philosophy of the program is based on the concept of "Organ Cross Talk"—the interconnected functioning of the body's systems. Each session is thoughtfully designed to promote internal harmony, encourage natural healing, and foster complete well-being.
